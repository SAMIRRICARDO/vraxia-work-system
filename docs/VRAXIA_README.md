## What is VRAXIA?

VRAXIA is a corporate AI Operating System that unifies specialized agents in a single enterprise infrastructure — enabling organizations to automate, scale, and preserve intelligence across every business unit.

**VRAXIA is not a chatbot.**  
**VRAXIA is not just automation.**

VRAXIA is the operating layer where AI agents, organizational knowledge, and corporate memory converge into a single, observable, modular platform.

## The Core Innovation: Human RAG

Traditional RAG retrieves documents.  
**Human RAG captures reasoning.**

Human RAG is VRAXIA's proprietary system for organizational knowledge preservation. It captures the reasoning patterns, judgment, and decision-making processes of high-performance professionals — encoding human expertise as a permanent, queryable organizational asset.

### The Problem It Solves

Every year, companies lose critical competitive advantage when key professionals leave. Their judgment, their accumulated expertise, their decision-making patterns — all of it disappears.

Human RAG exists to solve this.

### What Human RAG Does

- Preserves expert reasoning patterns, not just documents
- Accelerates onboarding by encoding institutional knowledge
- Reduces organizational knowledge loss
- Creates a living, evolving corporate memory
- Scales top-performer judgment across the organization

**The focus is not to replace people.**  
**The focus is to preserve and scale human knowledge.**

## PsychAgent

PsychAgent is the cognitive profiling engine inside Human RAG.

It performs cognitive and behavioral assessment through AI, building a calibrated psychological profile that increases the fidelity of each professional's digital twin.

**PsychAgent analyzes:**

- Personality inference
- Decision-making style
- Core values
- Behavior under pressure
- Cognitive biases
- Operational psychological profile

The result is a high-fidelity cognitive model that allows Human RAG to reproduce not just what a professional knows — but **how they think**.
