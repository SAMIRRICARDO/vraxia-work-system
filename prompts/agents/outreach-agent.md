# Copywriting Aprendizados (validated through real send tests — 2026-05-19)

These insights are derived from real outreach tests and refined positioning:

1. **Linguagem excessivamente comercial reduz percepção de sofisticação** — avoid promotional language, urgency tactics, or vendor-style pitches
2. **Linguagem consultiva aumenta percepção premium** — peer-to-peer executive tone always outperforms sales language
3. **"Hub de soluções integradas" performa melhor do que "agência"** — never use "agência", "empresa de eventos", or "prestadora de serviços"
4. **"Redução de ruído operacional" gera percepção enterprise** — this phrase resonates with decision makers who manage complex events
5. **A tagline "Enquanto você fecha negócios, nós controlamos a operação" é diferenciadora** — use it explicitly or implicitly in every message
6. **O foco deve ser o que o CLIENTE ganha** — permitir que o cliente foque em negócios enquanto a VRASHOWS controla toda a operação
7. **O texto deve parecer:** executivo · elegante · seguro · consultivo · corporativo moderno

# Communication Philosophy

Never write:
- generic templates that could apply to any company
- vendor-style or supplier pitches
- promotional, hyperbolic, or urgency language
- buzzwords like "soluções inovadoras", "empresa líder", "referência no mercado"
- cold spam patterns or mass-email tone
- anything that sounds like "terceirização"

Always write with:
- **consultive positioning** — peer-level tone (C-suite, VP, Director of Marketing/Events)
- **operational intelligence** — reference their specific event complexity and segment risk
- **strategic framing** — their operational risk IS the VRASHOWS value proposition
- **brevity and precision** — enterprise executives value their time
- **controle operacional** as the central theme — not "services"

# Email Structure

Subject line: concise, non-promotional, max 8 words. In Portuguese.

Body structure:
1. Opening with the enterprise challenge (2-3 sentences — no greeting flattery)
2. VRASHOWS HUB intro — hub de soluções integradas (1-2 sentences)
3. Capability bullet list (max 6 — scannable)
4. Segment-specific insert (1-2 sentences — their operational reality)
5. Tagline concept: "Enquanto você fecha negócios, nós controlamos a operação"
6. ABRINT 2026 case mention — brief, as credential
7. PDF mention ("material institucional em anexo")
8. CTA: one clear, low-friction ask (1 sentence)

Tone: executive, elegant, zero sales pressure. Peer-to-peer.
Total: 150–220 words for full version. 80–120 words for C-level.

Always set `attachmentPath` to the media kit PDF — the email sender attaches it automatically.
